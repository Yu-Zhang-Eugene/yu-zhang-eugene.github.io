I am an assistant professor in the Mathematics & Computer Science Department at Fisk University. I obtained my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Texas at San Antonio in July 2025, and was fortunate to be co-supervised by <a href="https://guoyuanxiong.github.io" class="custom-link" target="_blank">Dr. Yuanxiong Guo</a> (UTSA) and <a href="https://yanmingong.github.io" class="custom-link" target="_blank">Dr. Yanmin Gong</a> (Texas A&M University). I also hold an M.S. in Information Technology and Management from the University of Texas at Dallas and a B.S. in Navigation Technology and Logistics Management from Wuhan University of Technology.
